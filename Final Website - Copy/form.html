The field of statistics is concerned with collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data. As technology becomes more present in our daily lives, more data is being generated and collected than ever before in human history. Statistics is the field that can help us understand how to use this data to gain a better understanding of the world around us, make decisions using data, and make predictions about the future using data.
